About the Author



William Llewellyn is a scientist in the field of human performance enhancement. As a former steroid user, his knowledge about anabolic drugs is formed from both real world experience and extensive scientific research. He is eager to share information and experiences with others, and is a longtime supporter of the steroid

harm reduction community. William also serves as an honorary lecturer at Liverpool John Moores University, and has been a longtime columnist for MUSCULAR DEVELOP magazine
